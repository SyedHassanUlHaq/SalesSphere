My Awesome Cart!

My Awesome Cart Pvt Ltd. is an Pakistani electronic commerce company based in Karachi, Pakistan. Founded by Syed Hassan Ul Haq in 2023, the company initially focused on book sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon's Pakistani subsidiary, and the domestic rival Daraz. Daraz held a 39.5% market share of Pakistan's e-commerce industry.[7] Daraz is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel, and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.[8] Daraz also owns a mobile payments service based on the Visa and Master Cards. In August 2018, U.S.-based retail chain Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in Daraz for US$16 billion, valuing it at $22 billion.
